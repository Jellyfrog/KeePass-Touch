Touch ID

If your device supports Touch ID KeePass Touch can lock/unlock your database with it.
To do that follow these steps:

  1. Go to settings via the button on the bottom left
  2. Change the "Touch ID"-Switch to on.
  3. Go back to the database selection.
  4. Put in your password for the last time.
  5. Next time you open it, you will be prompted for your fingerprint for identification and your database unlocks.

To turn Touch ID off, just visit the settings again and change the switch back off.
