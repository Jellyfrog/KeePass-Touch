WiFi / WLAN Local Syncing

You can transfer databases locally and wirelessly in an easy way with KeePass Touch. To do so, follow these steps:

  1. Press the 2nd most left "Local Sync"-Button in the bottom bar.
  2. At the bottom of your list of databases an IP / address is shown, like e.g.
    http://xxx.xxx.xxx.xxx or
    http://***.iphone.local
  3. Open a browser of your choice on any device you want to sync to and insert that IP / address in the address bar.
  4. You will be presented with an interface that can upload, download, erase and rename a database of your choice.
